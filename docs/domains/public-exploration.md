# Public Exploration

## Goal
- Let public users move from animal type to disease to region-level livestock-health intelligence with minimal friction.

## In Scope
- landing and animal-selection entrypoints
- locale routing and switching for public flows
- disease catalog filtered by animal type
- public disease detail pages
- public KPIs
- public timeline and region filters
- source-labeled public activity summaries

## Constraints
- Public views may use official confirmed data, verified veterinary signals, and public-source intelligence, but never exact private coordinates or identity data.
- The public flow always starts with animal, then disease.
- Public experiences must be complete in English and Spanish.
- Public spatial views use administrative regions only.
- Low-volume regions are suppressed or delayed when anonymity rules require it.
