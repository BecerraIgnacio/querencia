# Reporting And Alerts

## Reporting
- Operational reports are confidential, not anonymous.
- Signal-bearing reports are submitted by verified veterinarians only.
- Stored reports retain source, confidence state, region, and network context.
- Report submission UI is bilingual, but user-authored free text stays in its original language.
- Unreviewed reports never flow directly into public outputs.

## Alerts
- Alerts are organization capabilities, not public capabilities.
- Watch areas and alerts are personal or organizational assets, not public data.
- Alert titles, descriptions, and settings must be available in English and Spanish.

## Cross-Cutting Rule
- Alert and dashboard logic must read from aggregated or policy-approved regional views.
- Public-source imports can inform awareness, but verified veterinary and official signals drive the operational trust model.
