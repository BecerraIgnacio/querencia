# ADR-003: Regional Privacy And Controlled Disclosure

## Status
- accepted

## Decision
- Public disease activity is rendered through administrative regions with anonymity thresholds and controlled disclosure rules.

## Why
- Territorial intelligence is useful only if reporter and farm privacy remain protected.

## Consequences
- Public maps, trends, and KPIs must not render exact private locations.
- Public outputs use regions, suppression thresholds, and delays instead of public hex cells.
- Operational reporting is confidential; public visibility is derived from approved regional views only.
- Shared privacy contracts are protected and reviewed before changes.
